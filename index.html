The Blessing of Walima

In Islam, the Walima is more than a celebration — it is a beautiful Sunnah of gratitude, joy, and togetherness as two lives begin their journey as one.

“Give a Walima, even if with one sheep.”

— Sahih al-Bukhari 5167

And just as the Prophet ﷺ invited others to share in this joy, we too wish to celebrate surrounded by those who hold a special place in our hearts.

Your presence, your duas,
and your blessings would make
our Walima truly complete.

Your presence will make our celebration even more special.

Please keep us in your duas as we begin this beautiful journey together.

May Allah bless this union and keep us together in goodness.

With Love,
Shayan & Juveria
